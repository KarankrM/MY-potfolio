ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rani Kumari, a passionate UI/UX Designer from India, just 16 years old and already deeply into the world of design. I've recently completed my class 10th from St. Christopher Mission School, and during my school years, I discovered my love for design — not through books, but through YouTube and my own curiosity.

I design using Figma and explore new creative ideas every day. With the help of tools like ChatGPT, I bring my imagination to life — crafting clean, user-friendly, and visually engaging interfaces. I've built multiple self-practice designs and continue to experiment with layouts, color palettes, and smooth animations to elevate user experience.

Although I haven't worked with real clients yet, people who see my work often call it "creative and unique", which motivates me to push even further. My dream? To start as a freelancer, build strong real-world experience, and eventually work with top tech companies like Apple or CRED, where design meets innovation.
