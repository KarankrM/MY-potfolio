CASE STUDY

PROJECT TYPE- THEMED ENTERTAINMENT APP

1. The Idea

This design was born right after I watched Deadpool 2. The movie's energy, chaos, and humor inspired me to explore a concept where a fictional entertainment app could live in Deadpool's world. To give it a modern and sleek look, I took inspiration from the layout and style of Apple's website — known for its minimal yet high-impact UI.

2. The Goal

My aim was to create a bold, satisfying, and user-friendly interface that felt like it belonged in a superhero universe — but still had real-world usability. I wanted it to look aesthetic and fun, yet functional.

3. The Process

  • Layout Inspiration: I took Apple's clean sections and recreated them with playful Deadpool-themed graphics.
  • Color & Mood: Red, black, and muted greys to match the Deadpool theme.
  • Visuals: Used large posters, clear call-to-actions, and bold fonts to give it that cinematic feel.
  • Interaction Hints: I imagined hover effects and subtle transitions to make every scroll feel dynamic.

4. The Outcome

The end result is a themed app UI concept that merges cinematic energy with structured design. This project helped me practice:

  • Designing from inspiration
  • Creating with themes
  • Matching color schemes to personality
  • Keeping layout clean while visuals are loud

5. What I Learned

  • Even bold, funny themes like Deadpool need balance in design
  • Inspiration is everywhere — even in movies and websites!
  • It's fun to make fictional ideas feel real through design
